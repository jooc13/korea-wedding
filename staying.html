Things to Do in Seoul 🇰🇷

Some ideas if you have extra time before or after the wedding.

Activities & Neighborhoods
Gyeongbokgung Palace
Seoul's most famous royal palace. Consider renting a traditional hanbok for photos and free palace admission.
www.royalpalace.go.kr
Bukchon Hanok Village
A beautiful neighborhood filled with traditional Korean homes, narrow alleyways, and great photo opportunities.
english.visitseoul.net
N Seoul Tower
Enjoy panoramic views of Seoul from one of the city's most recognizable landmarks.
www.seoultower.co.kr
Cheonggyecheon Stream
A scenic urban walking path that is especially beautiful in the evening.
english.visitseoul.net
Museum Kimchikan
Discover the history of Korea's most famous food and participate in kimchi-making experiences.
www.kimchikan.com/en
Ikseon-dong
A charming neighborhood where traditional architecture meets trendy cafés, restaurants, and boutiques.
Myeong-dong
Popular for shopping, skincare products, fashion, and street food.
Hongdae
Known for live music, nightlife, cafés, and a youthful atmosphere.
Gangnam
Upscale shopping, restaurants, and modern Seoul city life.
Starfield COEX Mall & Library
One of Seoul's largest shopping complexes, featuring the famous Starfield Library.
www.starfield.co.kr/coexmall
Half-Day / Full-Day Excursions
DMZ Tour
Visit the Korean Demilitarized Zone and learn about the history of the Korean Peninsula.
Nami Island
A picturesque destination known for its tree-lined paths and seasonal scenery.
www.namisum.com/eng
Suwon Hwaseong Fortress
A UNESCO World Heritage site located about an hour from Seoul.
english.visitkorea.or.kr
Han River Cruise
Relax and enjoy views of the Seoul skyline from the water.
